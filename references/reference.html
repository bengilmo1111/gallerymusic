Fees & Funding

Exceptional value music education

Our classes are heavily subsidised through the Ministry of Education's Out-of-Hours Arts and Music Programme, keeping costs accessible for all families.

Annual Tuition Fee
$155
per student, per year (all four terms)
  • One instrument class per week (30 mins)
  • All term dates included in the fee
  • Choir is free — no extra charge
  • Subsidised by the Ministry of Education
  • Managed via ClubHub for easy payments

Subsidised programme
Gallery Music Centre is part of the Ministry of Education's out-of-hours programme. This means classes are kept at a very low cost compared to private music tuition.

Sibling discounts
We welcome whole families — please contact us to discuss fees if you have multiple children enrolling.

Waitlists
Keyboard and guitar classes often have significant waitlists. We recommend getting in touch early. Orchestra and pre-instrumental classes may have spaces available — contact us to find out.

Payments
Fees are managed through ClubHub. Please keep your details up to date and use ClubHub to manage your family contributions.

How to enrol

We welcome new students throughout the year for some classes. Here's how to get started.

1

Check available classes

Review our classes list and check your child's age and experience. Note that keyboard and guitar have significant waitlists — get in touch early.

2

Contact us to express interest

Email or call us to let us know which class you're interested in. We'll let you know about availability and add you to the waitlist if needed.

3

Complete enrolment via ClubHub

We use ClubHub to manage enrolments and payments. You'll receive a link to complete your registration and set up your family contribution.

4

Start Saturday lessons!

Arrive at Avalon Primary School, 10 Gordon Street, Lower Hutt at your class start time. Please be ready outside the classroom a few minutes before.
