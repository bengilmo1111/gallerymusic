About the Orchestra

The Gallery Community Orchestra is open to musicians of all ages — unlike our other classes, which are primarily for primary and intermediate school students. Whether you're a teenager, a university student, or an adult returning to music, you're welcome here.

Players should have approximately 3 years of prior tuition on their instrument. The orchestra meets on Saturday mornings alongside the children's classes at Dyer Street School, making it easy for parents to play while their children are in lessons.

Rehearsals cover a range of repertoire from classical to contemporary, chosen to be engaging and achievable for players of varying abilities. The orchestra performs at the annual Gallery concert and occasionally at community events throughout the year.

Who Is It For?

The orchestra welcomes a wide range of players.

🎵

Returning Musicians

Adults who played an instrument years ago and want to pick it up again in a relaxed, supportive setting.

🎓

Progressing Students

Older students who have come through Gallery's programme and are ready for a more challenging ensemble experience.

🤝

Community Musicians

Anyone looking for a friendly, low-pressure group to make music with on Saturday mornings. All ages and backgrounds welcome.

No audition required — just a willingness to learn and play together. (Please confirm with admin.)
